# GuideFit — Safe Workout Guidance App

Welcome to **GuideFit**, a modern web app designed to help you train smarter and stay safe. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, GuideFit provides guided warm-ups, form cues, and cooldown routines to minimize injury risk and maximize your workout effectiveness.

## About GuideFit

GuideFit is built on the principle that proper preparation and recovery are just as important as the workout itself. Our app guides you through every stage of your training session:

- **Warm-up Sequences** — Dynamic 3-minute routines tailored to your workout type, preparing your body for peak performance.
- **Form Guidance** — Step-by-step visual and textual cues to ensure you maintain proper technique, protecting your joints and maximizing results.
- **Cooldown & Mobility** — Recovery routines and stretches designed to reduce soreness, improve flexibility, and aid muscle recovery.

## Why GuideFit?

- **Injury Prevention** — Structured guidance helps you avoid common mistakes and form errors that lead to injury.
- **Accessible** — Designed for all fitness levels, from absolute beginners to seasoned gym-goers.
- **Modern & Responsive** — Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- **Free & Fast** — No subscriptions or complicated setup—just open and start training safely.

## Getting Started

1. **Open the app** — Open `index.html` in your web browser.
2. **Choose your session** — Select from guided warm-ups, exercises, or cooldowns.
3. **Follow the guidance** — Read cues, watch form tips, and complete routines at your own pace.
4. **Track your progress** — Stay consistent and build better habits.

## Local Setup

To run GuideFit locally:

```bash
# Navigate to the project folder
cd /path/to/Guide-app-while-taking-work-out

# Start a simple HTTP server
python3 -m http.server 8000
```

Then open your browser and go to `http://localhost:8000`.
