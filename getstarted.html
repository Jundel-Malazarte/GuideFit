Get Started with GuideFit

Follow these simple steps to begin your safe workout journey and unlock your fitness potential with guidance every step of the way.

1

Create an Account

Sign up with your email to personalize your workout experience and track your progress over time.

2

Set Your Goals

Choose your fitness goals to receive tailored warm-up and cooldown routines designed for you.

3

Select Workout Type

Pick the type of workout you'll be doing (cardio, strength training, yoga, or more).

4

Start Your Session

Click "Start Guided Session" to begin your first workout with real-time safety guidance.

5

Follow the Prompts

Listen to audio cues and follow visual guides for proper form and technique throughout.

6

Cool Down

Follow the recommended cooldown routine to aid recovery and improve flexibility.
